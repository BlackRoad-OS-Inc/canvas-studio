Export

Export to Every Format

From web-optimized PNG to print-ready PDF, Canvas Studio exports exactly what you need at the quality you expect.

PNG

Lossless raster with transparency. 1x, 2x, 3x, or custom resolution. Perfect for web and social.

SVG

Scalable vector graphics. Resolution-independent. Editable in any vector tool. Ideal for logos and icons.

PDF

Print-ready at 300 DPI. CMYK color space support. Bleed and crop marks. Multi-page documents.

JPEG

Compressed raster with adjustable quality. 70-100% quality slider. Best for photography-heavy designs.

WebP

Next-gen web format. 30% smaller than PNG. Transparency support. Fastest page load times.

GIF

Animated exports for simple motion graphics. Up to 60 frames. Optimized color palettes.
